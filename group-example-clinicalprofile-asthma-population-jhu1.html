Group-example-clinicalprofile-asthma-population-jhu1

FHIR Infrastructure Work GroupMaturity Level: N/ABallot Status: InformativeCompartments: Device, Patient, Practitioner

This is the narrative for the resource. See also the XML, JSON or Turtle format.

Patients having a diagnosis of asthma recorded on the problem list or as an encounter diagnosis.

All patients seen as either outpatients or inpatients at Johns Hopkins Hospital, JHCP, and Bayview between 3/1/2016 and 3/1/2018 having a diagnosis of asthma recorded on the problem list or as an encounter diagnosis.
