11.9 Resource MedicinalProductClinicals - Content

Biomedical Research and Regulation Work GroupMaturity Level: 0 Trial Use Compartments: Not linked to any defined compartments

The clinical particulars - indications, contraindications etc. of a medicinal product, including for regulatory purposes.

11.9.1 Scope and Usage

A set of clinically relevent aspects relating to the potential uses and problems associated with a medicinal product
